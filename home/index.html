About Us

Start-up Achiever is a blockchain-based crowdfunding platform that connects investors directly to start-ups without the use of a middleman. It eliminates centralized authority systems and payment transfer between two parties to make the entire process more decentralized. It enables any start-up to raise money by allowing them to upload their pitches and develop smart contracts that allow contributors to govern the money invested and project creators and investors to efficiently raise and reserve financing. To maintain the uniqueness of the project, it is recommended to test for plagiarism by taking the project description from the project manager or entrepreneur.
